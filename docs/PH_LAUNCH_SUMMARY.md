## Critical Success Factors

1. **Authentic Engagement**
   - Respond to every comment in first 6 hours
   - Use founder's voice (not marketing speak)
   - Show genuine curiosity about user feedback

2. **Network Effect**
   - 300+ people to reach out to on launch day
   - Personal messages > mass emails
   - Ask for specific feedback, not just upvotes

3. **System Stability**
   - 99.8%+ uptime (no errors on launch day)
   - Monitor Grafana dashboards in real-time
   - Have DevOps on standby for critical issues

4. **Content Consistency**
   - Daily updates for first week
   - Show shipping based on feedback
   - Post weekly for month 1

5. **Community First**
   - Focus on helping, not selling
   - Offer free tier to thoughtful commenters
   - Build relationships, not just conversions

## Common Objections (Pre-Written Answers)

### "Is this legal?"
"Yes! Electricity is deregulated in 20+ US states. You can switch suppliers
like switching phone carriers. Completely legal and transparent."

### "Won't my power go out?"
"No. Same physical infrastructure. Switching takes 3-5 days, you never lose power.
Utilities are regulated to ensure continuity."

### "How do you make money?"
"Free tier is unlimited. Pro ($4.99/mo) = recommendations. Business ($14.99/mo) = API access.
We only profit if you save money."

### "Why is electricity sometimes cheaper elsewhere?"
"Deregulation creates competition. Each supplier sets their own rates.
Rates also vary by time-of-use, contract length, and demand."

## Final Reminders

1. **Be authentic.** PH community smells BS. Write like a real founder.

2. **Engage relentlessly.** First 6 hours are critical. Answer every comment.

3. **Show gratitude.** Thank people by name. Genuine appreciation > money.

4. **Ship fast.** If someone asks for feature X, ship it by day 3.

5. **Build community.** Focus on helping, not selling. Conversions follow naturally.

6. **Share metrics.** Transparency builds trust. Show upvote count, signup count, your actual NPS.

7. **Plan for success.** If you hit top 10, expect 500+ signups/day. Make sure app scales.

8. **Think long-term.** PH launch is day 1 of 365. Build relationships for future support.
